Welcome

Medication adherence is a global issue identified by the World Health Organization as a problem that the health care community must address. Yet, we (as researchers and health care providers) do not have a sound definition of what it means to be "adherent" at a granular level. This project seeks to understand what medication adherence means to prescribers. If we can develop a more rigourous definition of adherence, then we can begin to develop tools to help patients maintain their medication adherence and ultimately have better outcomes.
