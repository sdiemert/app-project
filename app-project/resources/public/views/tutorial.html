Tutorial

The following is a practice question to familiarize you with the user interface for this study. Subsequent study questions will use a similar format.

The timeline below represents a hypothetical patient's medication taking behavior. The axis represents time, shown in hours since the beginning of the prescription (when it was issued). Points in time at which the patient consumed their medication are shown as dots with the dose shown directly above.

Your task is to indicate a range of time when the patient may administer the next dose of medication. I.e. if the next dose was administered within that range you (as their physician) would consider it to be "adherent".

Consider the following prescription: take blueberricillin 10 mg once daily. Suppose that you think the next dose could be taken been 50 hours and 70 hours.

Adjust the interval sliders to indicate that third dose should be taken between 50 and 70 hours. Press "Done" when you are finished.
