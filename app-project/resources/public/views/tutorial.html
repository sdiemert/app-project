Tutorial


This tutorial is meant to familiarize you with the interface used for the rest of the study.

Given a patient's medication administration history (shown on a timeline), we would like you to indicate when they could take their next dose (using the sliders on the timeline) and still be considered "adherent". Use the green slider to denote the first time at which the medication should be administered and the red slider to indicate the last time at which the medication should be administered.

As part of this tutorial, we would like you adjust the sliders to indicate that the patient's next (their third) dose could be taken on day 2 between 8:00 and 20:00. Use the timeline below. When you are finished, click "Done" to proceed".
